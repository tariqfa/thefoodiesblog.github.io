Bonchon Review

Now this is a place that I can get behind. Food is really good and that considering this is not a massive franchise with millions of dollars to spend
I love the chicken katsu, and there wings are really good. Most companies should take notes from Bonchon. Score 9/10



Hibachi Box Review:

Hibachi box is one of those places where I have a love hate relationship with. Some days it really makes my day, everything is perfect.
On other days it is extermly salty and soggy. You really have to pick your battles with this place, because all though the food is exceptional most of the time,
it can prove otherwise the other half of the time. Score: 7/10


Mcdonalds Review:

The Mcdonalds here in Richmond Virginia leaves a lot to be desired.
the food takes forever to get to you and to top it off your order is likely to be wrong.
overall poor review. final rating: 4 out of 10
